ABOUT BIOMAG

Who are we?

BioMag is a group of STEM enthusiasts who come together to communicate science in a way that it hasn't been communicated at Fordham. A way, we hope, that is both effective and fun, and that brings the STEM and non-STEM communities closer. Once each semester, we publish our magazine BioMag to highlight current issues in science.
